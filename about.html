About Bolzano

Where Tradition Meets Passion

Our Story

Nestled in the heart of the city, Bolzano was founded in 2005 by Marco and Sofia Rossi, a husband-and-wife duo with a shared love for authentic Italian cuisine. Hailing from the picturesque town of Bolzano in Northern Italy, they brought with them generations of family recipes and a passion for creating unforgettable dining experiences.

Our restaurant is named after the charming town of Bolzano, known for its rich culinary heritage and breathtaking Alpine landscapes. At Bolzano, we strive to bring a slice of Italy to your table, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Our Mission

At Bolzano, our mission is simple: to create dishes that warm the heart and soul. We believe that food is more than just sustenance—it’s a way to connect, celebrate, and share joy. Every dish we serve is crafted with love, care, and a commitment to authenticity.

Signature Dishes

Our menu is a tribute to the diverse flavors of Italy. From our hand-tossed Margherita Pizza to our slow-cooked Osso Buco, each dish tells a story. Don’t miss our Tiramisu, a family recipe passed down through generations, or our Homemade Gnocchi, served with a rich tomato basil sauce.

Join us at Bolzano and experience the true taste of Italy. We can’t wait to welcome you!
